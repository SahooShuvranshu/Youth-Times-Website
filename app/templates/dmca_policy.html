DMCA Takedown Policy

Youth Times is DMCA protected. We respect intellectual property rights and respond promptly to valid DMCA takedown notices. If you believe your copyrighted work has been used without permission, please follow the process below.

How to File a DMCA Notice

  • Identify the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed.
  • Provide the specific URL(s) where the infringing content appears.
  • Include your contact information and a statement of good faith belief.
  • Sign the notice electronically or physically.
  • Attach any supporting documentation or evidence.

Send DMCA Notices to:

Email: contact.youthtimes@gmail.com
Subject Line: DMCA Takedown Notice
Response Time: 48-72 hours

Counter-Notification Process

If you believe content was removed in error, you may file a counter-notification using the same contact email above. Please include all relevant details and supporting evidence.

Additional Information

  • We review all DMCA notices carefully and act in accordance with applicable law.
  • We may contact you for further information or clarification.
  • All communications are kept confidential.

Note: Filing false DMCA claims may result in legal consequences. Ensure your claim is valid before submitting.
