Privacy Policy

Youth Times is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

  • Email address (for account management, communication, and notifications)
  • Profile information (bio, location, website, profile image)
  • Articles, comments, and other content you submit
  • Usage analytics, device information, and cookies
  • Feedback and support requests

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our journalism platform
  • To communicate with you about your account, updates, and support
  • To improve our services, user experience, and site security
  • To ensure content quality and combat misinformation
  • To comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We may share data only when required by law, to protect our rights or users' safety, or with your explicit consent. Third-party services (e.g., analytics, hosting) may process data as needed for site operation.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and regular audits.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Contact us for privacy concerns or questions

To exercise your rights or for any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at contact.youthtimes@gmail.com.
