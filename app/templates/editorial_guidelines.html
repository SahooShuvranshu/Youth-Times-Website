Editorial Guidelines

Standards and practices for Youth Times contributors

Our Mission

Youth Times is dedicated to providing a platform for young voices to share their perspectives, experiences, and insights with the world. We believe in the power of authentic storytelling and the importance of diverse viewpoints in shaping meaningful discourse.

Content Standards

✓ We Accept:

  • Original, well-researched articles
  • Personal essays and opinion pieces
  • News coverage and reporting
  • Creative writing and poetry
  • Reviews (books, movies, events)
  • Interviews and profiles

✗ We Don't Accept:

  • Plagiarized or non-original content
  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Defamatory or libelous material
  • Content promoting illegal activities
  • Spam or promotional material

Writing Standards

Length Requirements

  • News Articles: 300-800 words
  • Opinion Pieces: 500-1200 words
  • Features: 800-2000 words
  • Reviews: 400-800 words

Style Guide

  • Write in clear, accessible language
  • Use active voice when possible
  • Fact-check all claims and statistics
  • Include proper attribution for quotes
  • Proofread for grammar and spelling

Submission Process

1

Register for a Youth Times account to submit articles.

2

Submit Article

Use our submission form with title, content, and category.

3

Editorial Review

Our team reviews submissions within 3-5 business days.

4

Publication

Approved articles are published and you'll receive notification.

Editorial Ethics

Accuracy & Verification

All factual claims must be verifiable. Include sources and links where appropriate.

Bias & Fairness

Present multiple perspectives on controversial topics. Clearly distinguish between news and opinion.

Privacy & Consent

Obtain consent before including personal information about individuals.

Corrections

We promptly correct errors and update articles when necessary.

Questions About Guidelines?

Our editorial team is here to help you create great content.
