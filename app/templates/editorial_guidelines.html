Editorial Guidelines

Youth Times maintains high editorial standards to ensure credible, ethical journalism. All contributors are expected to follow these guidelines for quality and integrity.

Fact-Checking Process

  • All articles undergo automated credibility scoring and editorial review
  • Sources must be verified, cited, and clearly attributed
  • Claims require evidence and supporting documentation
  • Editorial review for sensitive or controversial topics
  • Corrections are issued promptly when errors are found

Writing Standards

  • Clear, accurate, and engaging prose
  • Objective reporting with minimal bias
  • Proper grammar, spelling, and style
  • Appropriate tone for target audience
  • Respectful language and inclusive representation

Source Requirements

  • Primary sources preferred over secondary
  • Multiple sources for controversial claims
  • Expert opinions from qualified individuals
  • Transparency about conflicts of interest
  • Disclosure of funding or sponsorships

Ethics Policy

  • No plagiarism, fabrication, or misrepresentation
  • Respect for privacy and dignity of all individuals
  • Fair representation of all viewpoints
  • Correction of errors promptly
  • Compliance with all legal and ethical standards

Editorial Independence

Our editorial decisions are made independently, free from commercial or political influence. Editorial integrity is our highest priority.

Contact

For editorial questions or feedback, contact contact.youthtimes@gmail.com.
